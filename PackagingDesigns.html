Packaging
Designs

When approaching packaging design I think about what packaging I’m drawn to when shopping.
I think anything with a bit of extra effort and illustrative flair tends to catch my attention.
I like when even the simplest item, like a can or a box, becomes a small piece of art to brighten
your day whenever you see it in your home.

This project is a personal one where I designed an entire brand of craft beer called ‘Noplace Brewery’. I love looking at, trying and collecting various craft beer cans so this was a dream project for me. I think craft beer can design can get really experimental so I thought having a theme of utopias and dystopias for my fictional beer brand would work really well to create psychedelic style designs. I did half utopian designs, inspired by nature and excess, and half dystopian designs, inspired by rampant technology and destruction.

I enjoyed the process of developing ideas for this project, as I started with a lot of automatic drawings and random
word prompts to try to create unexpected outcomes. This led to some interesting shapes and concepts.
I also researched into retrofuturistic artwork from the 1970s and iconic cyberpunk/dystopian films from the past to
feed into my practice.
