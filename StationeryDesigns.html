Designs

You’ll also see some vinyl sticker designs that I created from doodles in my personal sketchbook, as well as some collaged Christmas cards. These are for an Etsy shop which I enjoy designing for since it’s fun to think about my illustrations in different contexts, like how they can turn an everyday product into something cheerful to brighten someone’s day!

Stationery has been a lifelong love of mine. So I always jump at the chance to design my own. I especially enjoyed
designing this letter writing set as I have spent many years trying to find the perfect ones to use myself. I designed
this in the way that you can use two complimentary writing papers in one letter and even seal it with a colourful
sticker! This stationery set and the notebook designs alongside, are inspired by aboriginal Australian folklore. This
started as a uni project called ‘folklore’ which I interpreted in my own way. I loved creating endless patterns that
I then collaged into little feathers and scales to make up birds and snakes.
